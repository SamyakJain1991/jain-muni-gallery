मुनि संघ का विवरण

  • नाम: उपाध्याय श्री विरंजन सागर जी
  • जन्म तिथि: 12/12/1981
  • जन्मस्थल: ग्राम सदगुआन, जिला दमोश, मध्य प्रदेश
  • Chullak Diksha: 13/02/2006
  • Muni Diksha: 18/01/2016
  • Info: A dedicated Jain Muni who has traveled across India spreading the message of Ahimsa and Jain teachings.
  • Childhood Memories: Young Ballu (Kamlesh) was very fond of cricket, playing in the village field with friends. Skilled in batting and bowling, he was an expert at hitting long shots, often putting pressure on the opposing team. Even today, his bat says, "The field has changed, but the game hasn’t.
